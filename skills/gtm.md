## GTM Framework

### 1. Market Definition

**Target segment** (pick one primary, one secondary — not "everyone"):
- Primary: Who is the ideal customer? Job title, company size, industry, buying trigger.
- Secondary: Who else will buy, but isn't the focus?
- Anti-target: Who explicitly should NOT be your customer right now?

**Market size sanity check:**
- TAM / SAM / SOM (rough order of magnitude is enough)
- Is this a new category, an existing category you're disrupting, or a feature of something larger?
